Common questions

Does the app collect my data?

No. Star Lore stores a minimal set of data on your device only (for example an optional display name and gameplay statistics). There are no analytics SDKs, advertising, or tracking tools. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Do I need an account?

No. Star Lore does not use accounts or cloud storage.

Is the app free?

Yes. The app is free and has no in‑app purchases or subscriptions.

Troubleshooting

  • Ensure you are on the latest version of the app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Fully close and reopen the app.
  • If needed, reinstall the app. This will reset on‑device data.
  • On web, clear site data/storage for the app’s domain in your browser settings.

Report a bug

When you email a bug report, please add:

  • Steps to reproduce the problem.
  • What you expected to happen and what happened instead.
  • Screenshots or a short screen recording, if possible.
  • Device model and operating system version.
